DRiFT: Differentiable Grid-Based Rigid–Fluid Coupling for Training and Control

Abstract

We introduce DRiFT, a differentiable grid-based method for fluid–rigid-body interaction enabling gradient-based control, trajectory optimization, and inverse simulation. Our method supports stable and efficient backward passes and couples variational pressure solves with rigid-body dynamics.
